## <Icon icon="trophy" /> Fantasy Sports Applications

Build fantasy padel games using real player statistics, match results, and rankings. Track player performance across tournaments to calculate fantasy points and leaderboards.

<Tip>
  Fantasy games typically use the **Players**, **Matches**, **Rankings**, and **Statistics** endpoints to calculate points based on real match performance.
</Tip>

**Example:**

## <Icon icon="chart-line" /> Sports Analytics & Research

Analyze player performance trends, head-to-head records, and ranking progressions. Generate insights for coaches, players, or sports researchers.

<Note>
  Combine **Statistics** with **Matches** data to build comprehensive performance reports and predictive models.
</Note>

**Relevant endpoints:** Players, Statistics, Rankings, Matches

---

## <Icon icon="newspaper" /> Sports Media & Journalism

Pull statistics for articles, generate match previews, and create data-driven recaps. Access historical data to provide context for your coverage.

<Info>
  Use tournament and match data to automatically generate pre-match previews and post-match summaries for your publication.
</Info>

**Relevant endpoints:** Players, Matches, Tournaments, Statistics

---

## <Icon icon="bullseye-arrow" /> Brand Sponsorships

Help brands and sponsors track the performance of their sponsored players. Monitor rankings, tournament results, and visibility metrics to measure sponsorship ROI.

<Tip>
  Use **Rankings** and **Matches** data to generate performance reports for sponsors, showing their players' progression and tournament achievements.
</Tip>

**Relevant endpoints:** Players, Matches, Rankings, Tournaments
