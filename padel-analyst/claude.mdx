This guide shows you how to install Padel Analyst as a connector in [Claude.ai](https://claude.ai), allowing you to query professional padel data directly in your conversations.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Claude Pro/Team Account" icon="user">
    Connectors require a Claude Pro or Team subscription
  </Card>
  <Card title="Padel API Token" icon="key">
    Get your token from the [API Tokens](https://padelapi.org/user/api-tokens) page
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Installation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Claude Settings">
    Click on your profile icon in the bottom-left corner of Claude and select **Settings** from the menu.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/claude/claude-step-1.webp" alt="Claude settings menu" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Connectors">
    In the Settings panel, click on **Connectors** in the left sidebar. Then click the **Add custom connector** button.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/claude/claude-step-2.webp" alt="Connectors settings page" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Padel Analyst">
    In the "Add custom connector" dialog, enter the following details:

    | Field | Value |
    |-------|-------|
    | **Name** | `Padel Analyst` |
    | **URL** | `https://padelapi.org/mcp?token=YOUR_API_TOKEN` |

    Replace `YOUR_API_TOKEN` with your actual API token from the [API Tokens page](https://padelapi.org/user/api-tokens).

    Click **Add** to save the connector.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/claude/claude-step-3.webp" alt="Add custom connector dialog" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Permissions (Optional)">
    After adding the connector, you can configure tool permissions. Click on **Padel Analyst** in your connectors list to see all available tools.

    Select **Always allow** from the dropdown to skip permission prompts for each tool, or leave the default settings to approve tools individually.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/claude/claude-optional-4.webp" alt="Tool permissions configuration" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Using Padel Analyst">
    In any chat, click the **+** button and hover over **Add from Padel Analyst** to enable the connector. Make sure the **Padel Analyst** toggle is turned on.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/claude/claude-step-5.webp" alt="Enable Padel Analyst in chat" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Padel Analyst

Once installed, you can ask Claude questions about professional padel. When Claude needs to fetch data, you'll see a permission request (unless you enabled "Always allow").

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/claude/claude-optional-6.webp" alt="Permission request for Padel Analyst" />
</Frame>

Click **Allow once** or **Always allow** to let Claude access the padel data.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Connector not appearing">
    Make sure you have a Claude Pro or Team subscription. Free accounts don't have access to custom connectors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Authentication errors">
    Verify that your API token is correct and that you have an active Analyst plan subscription.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Tools not working">
    Check that the connector is enabled in your chat (the toggle should be on). Also verify your token hasn't expired.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
